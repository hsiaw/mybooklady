The Bellevue Children's Academy Pre-Primary Book Fair!

Order books through the link on this page from March 20 - April 3 to support your school!

You can purchase books for your own family or donate to your child's classroom. See links below for Teacher Wish Lists.

The best part? Bellevue Children's Academy will receive 50% back in FREE BOOKS for their classrooms and library based on sales from this book fair! Not only are you supporting literacy and the love of books for your own children, you are spreading the joy of reading at Bellevue Children's!
