The Bellevue Children's Academy Book Fair!

March 20 - April 3

Greetings Students, Parents and Faculty!!
Did you know? Usborne Books & More is now called PaperPie? Our name has changed but we’re still home to amazing Usborne and Kane Miller books, and with our recent acquisition of SmartLab Toys, we’re now offering engaging STEM activity kits!
Ready for some book fair fun?
This year parents are invited to peruse the book fair display in person! Book fair hours:

Monday 3/20 3:00pm-5:30pm
Tuesday 3/21 8:00 am-9:00am & 3:00pm-5:30pm
Wednesday 3/22 8:00am-9:00am & 3:00pm-5:30pm
Thursday 3/23 8:00am-4:00pm
Friday 3/24 8:00am-4:00pm

Can’t make it in? Browse the curated book fair list below.
Order through the secure shopping link on this page. If you’re interested in helping teachers build their classroom libraries, scroll down to see classroom wish lists.
Orders will be processed immediately and shipped to you next day.
Expect to receive your new books within 7 to 9 days! Every purchase benefits BCA2! Your school will receive 50 percent of total sales back in free books!
Fun Fact: Our new PaperPie shipping boxes UNFOLD into a playmat/town children can color!


Want book recommendations? Email me pamela@mybooklady.com with your child’s age and interests and I’ll send you a curated list of books.
