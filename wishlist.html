How to make a wishlist

Step 1: Make sure you are in the correct event, then register for an account. If you already have an account just login.

Step 2: Click on any book in the shop to get to it's shop page. From there you can add it to your wishlist.

Step 3: To check your wishlist, click on the wishlist panel in the top left of the shop page.

Step 4: From this page, select view your personal wishlist.

Step 5: From here you can view your wishlist and make changes.
